# Home Screen Blueprint

## Purpose
The Home screen is the command centre of Retention Radar. Every visit should end with the user feeling organised, confident and clear on their next actions.

## North Star Questions
1. What should I do next?
2. Who needs me today?

## Emotional Goal
The user should feel calm, in control and prepared—not overwhelmed.

## Layout
### Header
- Greeting
- Current date
- Optional AI voice button

### Today's Priorities (Primary Section)
A ranked list of the highest-value actions.
Each card shows:
- Client name
- Recommended action
- Why it matters
- Estimated time
- Expected impact

### Who Needs Me Today?
Visual list of clients needing attention, grouped by priority.

### Relationship Health Snapshot
- Healthy
- Watch
- Needs Attention
Displayed visually without creating alarm.

### AI Workspace
Users can ask natural-language questions, including voice:
- Who should I contact today?
- Why is Sarah high priority?
- What changed this week?

## Design Rules
- Show actions before analytics.
- Show explanations before raw data.
- Never display an alert without a recommendation.
- Reduce clicks by surfacing the next best action immediately.

## Success Criteria
A user should know their priorities within 30 seconds of opening the app.
