# Landing Page Specification

## Objective
Build a premium, conversion-focused landing page for Retention Radar that makes potential customers and investors immediately understand the product, trust the positioning, and book a research call.

## Primary Audience
- Service-based business owners
- Agency owners
- Potential investors

## Primary CTA
Book a 20-minute Research Call

## Secondary CTA
Watch Demo — Coming Soon

---

## Core Message
Retention Radar helps service-based businesses keep more clients by showing who needs attention, why they need attention, and what to do next.

## Hero Headline
Know who needs your attention before clients slip away.

## Hero Subheadline
Retention Radar is an AI relationship intelligence workspace that helps service businesses organise client relationships, prioritise follow-ups, and take the right action before retention problems become expensive.

## Hero Visual
Animated digital radar inspired by the Retention Radar logo.

### Radar Requirements
- Dark circular radar interface
- Neon blue/cyan sweep arm
- Rotating detection line
- Client dots positioned around the radar
- Dots pulse when detected
- Glow fades after the sweep passes
- Subtle grid background
- Smooth premium animation
- Must feel sleek, not childish

---

Most clients do not leave without warning.

The warning signs are usually there. They are just scattered across memory, emails, messages, meetings and missed follow-ups.

Retention Radar turns relationship noise into daily priorities.

Instead of forcing owners to mentally track every relationship, Retention Radar organises client signals into a clear workspace that answers two questions: what should I do next, and who needs me today?

## Design Direction
- Dark mode first
- Neon blue and cyan accents
- Subtle electric green highlights
- Glassmorphism cards
- Premium SaaS feel
- Clean typography
- Smooth Framer Motion animations
- Mobile responsive
- Fast loading
- Accessible contrast

## Technical Stack
- Next.js
- React
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Framer Motion

## Build Constraints
- Build only the landing page and reusable landing-page components.
- Do not build login, dashboard, backend, database or AI logic yet.
- Use placeholder links for CTAs until booking link is provided.
- Keep the code clean, scalable and easy to extend.

## Success Criteria
The page succeeds if a service business owner can understand within 30 seconds:
1. What Retention Radar does.
2. Why it matters.
3. Why it is not just another CRM.
4. What action to take next.
