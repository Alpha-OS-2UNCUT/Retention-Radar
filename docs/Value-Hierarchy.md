# Retention Radar Value Hierarchy

## Core Philosophy
Customers do not buy Retention Radar because it uses AI. They buy clarity, confidence and consistency in managing client relationships.

## What Customers Buy
- Clarity
- Confidence
- Organisation
- Consistent prioritisation
- Peace of mind

## What Retention Radar Delivers
- Relationship intelligence
- Consistent action recommendations
- Visual client mapping
- Prioritised workflows
- AI that augments human judgement rather than replacing it

## Business Outcomes
- Better client retention
- Higher client lifetime value
- Stronger relationships
- More referrals
- Increased recurring revenue

## Product Principles
1. Decision-first, not data-first.
2. Never present a problem without a recommended next action.
3. AI augments human judgement; it does not replace it.
4. Retention Radar should reduce cognitive load by making every client relationship visible and organised.
5. Recommendation quality should remain consistent regardless of workload or number of clients.
6. Retention is the outcome; relationship clarity is the mechanism.
