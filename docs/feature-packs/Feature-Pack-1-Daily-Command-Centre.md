# Feature Pack 1 — Daily Command Centre

## Objective
Create a morning workspace that helps business owners understand exactly what to do next in under 30 seconds.

## Success Statement
When a user opens Retention Radar they should immediately feel:
- Calm
- Organised
- Confident
- Ready to act

## Components

### 1. Welcome Header
- Personal greeting
- Date
- Optional AI voice entry point

### 2. What Should I Do Next?
The primary focus of the application.
Shows a ranked list of the highest-value actions.

### 3. Who Needs Me Today?
Displays clients requiring attention, grouped by priority and accompanied by concise reasoning.

### 4. AI Analyst
Supports text and future voice conversations.
Examples:
- What changed this week?
- Why is this client a priority?
- Who has improved?

### 5. Relationship Health Snapshot
A calm visual overview using Healthy, Watch and Needs Attention states rather than alarming language.

### 6. Today's Progress
Tracks completed recommendations and remaining priorities.

## Design Principles
- Actions before analytics.
- Explanations before data.
- Confidence before urgency.
- Every recommendation includes reasoning.
- Every recommendation is actionable.

## Validation Goal
After using this screen for one week, a founder should feel they are making more consistent client decisions with less mental effort.
