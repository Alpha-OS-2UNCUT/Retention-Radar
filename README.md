# Retention Radar

## Mission
Help service-based businesses keep more clients by identifying who is most at risk of leaving and recommending what to do before it is too late.

## Who it is for
Service-based businesses that rely on long-term, repeat client relationships.

Examples:
- Marketing agencies
- SEO agencies
- Recruitment agencies
- Consultants
- Accountancy firms
- Software agencies

## The problem
Businesses lose valuable clients because warning signs are missed, relationships are not prioritised consistently, and there is no clear view of client health over time.

## The solution
Retention Radar continuously prioritises clients by risk, tracks relationship health over time, and recommends the next best action before clients leave.
